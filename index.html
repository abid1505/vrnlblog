What Are Match Odds in a Betting Exchange?

Match odds are the most basic and popular market in any betting exchange. This market allows users to bet on the outcome of a match — whether Team A wins, Team B wins, or the match ends in a draw (if applicable).

Unlike traditional sportsbooks, betting exchanges allow users to bet against each other, meaning you can either:

• Back a team (bet that they will win), or
• Lay a team (bet that they will not win).

This unique format gives you more control, better odds, and the opportunity to act like a bookmaker.

What is the Winner Market in a Betting Exchange?

The Winner Market in a betting exchange refers to betting on the overall winner of a tournament, series, or competition — not just a single match. It's one of the most popular futures or outright markets in the exchange betting world.

Example : You're betting on the IPL 2025 Winner Market:

• Backing Royal Challengers Bangalore at odds of 7.0 means if they win, you get 7x your stake.

• Laying Mumbai Indians means you’re betting against them winning the tournament.
